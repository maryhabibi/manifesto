Ethical Virtual Experiences and Transparent Advertising Manifesto

In a tech-dominated world, we advocate for ethical living and are committed to responsibly integrating virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR) into our lives. We aim to reveal the truths of virtual existence, especially in everyday life and advertising. Vision We envision VR/AR as a tool for genuine connection, education, and empowerment, not corporate manipulation. Every individual has the right to experience VR/AR authentically. Let VR/AR liberate, not manipulate, with immersive experiences grounded in authenticity.

Authentic Connection

Our commitment extends to transparency in advertising within the VR/AR space. We advocate against deceptive marketing practices that exploit the immersive nature of these technologies. Users deserve to navigate virtual spaces free from hidden agendas, ensuring that marketing messages are clear, honest, and aligned with their expectations. Transparency is paramount, and we call for industry-wide adherence to ethical advertising standards.

Education and Empowerment

VR should be a conduit for knowledge, learning, and personal empowerment, not just escapism. We reject practices that exploit users for profit and advocate for a virtual world that uplifts and inspires.

Inclusivity

Inclusivity

Inclusivity is a crucial aspect for VR/AR technologies. These technologies should embrace diversity, ensuring that virtual environments are welcoming to people from all backgrounds, cultures, and abilities. By actively opposing discriminatory practices within virtual spaces, VR/AR can foster a sense of belonging for users regardless of their race, gender, ethnicity, or physical capabilities. Inclusive design principles should be integrated to create virtual experiences that cater to a broad and varied audience, promoting a more equitable and respectful use of VR/AR technologies.

Call to Action

Call to Action

We urge VR/AR developers, content creators, and users to uphold these principles for a responsible VR future that enriches lives and respects ethical standards.
